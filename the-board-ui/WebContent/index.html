The Board!

If you are reading this, you are a stakeholder of The Board - the online version of the board that once adorned the wall of the proud Powell Indoor Arena.

Work on the board will take place over several iterations, with a deliverable due to you, the stakeholders, at the end of each iteration.

IterationStart DateEnd DateDeliverable
1February 8thFebruary 28thUI that displays games/predictions, no UX yet. All predictions will have to be communicated to Richard for input.
2February 29thMarch 13tdUser ability to enter games. No security, therefore at this time anyone will be able to predict on behalf of others. Much fun will be had here.
3March 14thApril 3rdLogin and security added, only for existing stakeholders.
4April 4thApril 24thStyling.
5April 25thMay 15thAbility for guests to enter predictions.
